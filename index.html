The SF Coffee Club

Who We Are

We are people who love coffee but we also love each other just as much! If you or a friend are looking for a place to be yourself and talk about anything you'd like with no judgement, this club is for you. We meet up at our favorite coffee shop in the city every week and talk about whatever is on our mind.

And the best part (other than the coffee)? It's completely free! Just pop by and we'll be here with open arms! We hope to see you at The Roast Coffee Shop, our sponsor.

"My favorite part of the week is being able to drink coffee with the best group of people! This club has made me feel like I belong somewhere."

Caitlin McGuire. - Frequent Coffee Drinker Since 2018

Come Join The Fun!
